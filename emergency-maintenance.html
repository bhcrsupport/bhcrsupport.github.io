EMERGENCY MAINTENANCE

OUR AFTER-HOURS EMERGENCY MAINTENANCE SERVICE IS AVAILABLE FOR ALL TENANTS OF BOONE HIGH COUNTRY RENTALS. IT ALLOWS US TO RESPOND TO AND REMEDIATE ANY MAJOR MAINTENANCE ISSUES THAT MAY OCCUR DURING THE HOURS THAT OUR OFFICE IS NOT OPEN.

WHAT IS A MAINTENANCE EMERGENCY?

SO WHAT IS A MAINTENANCE EMERGENCY?

MAINTENANCE EMERGENCY

- No running water (frozen pipes, busted pipes, well water issues, etc.)

-Major water leaks that will cause water damage to property (overflowing dishwashers/washers, busted pipes, etc.)

- No heat during cold weather events (broken heating systems)
*NOTE: If you do not have heat because you forgot to get your fuel refilled then that is NOT a maintenance emergency. All tenants are required to keep their own fuel tanks filled up and ready to go for heating purposes.
