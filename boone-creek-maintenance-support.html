MAINTENANCE INFORMATION

POWER

New River Light & Power provides the electric service to Boone Creek. If you have a power outage or issue please call them at 828-264-8621. For more information please visit their website at https://nrlp.appstate.edu/ or stop by their customer service office at 220 Winklers Creek Road, Boone, NC 28607.

WATER

The Town of Boone supplies the water for Boone Creek. If you have a water outage or issue please call them at 828-268-6250. For more information please visit their website at http://www.townofboone.net/departments/public-services/ or stop by their customer service office at 321 East King Street, Boone, NC 28607.

TRASH

Republic Services handles all trash/waste disposal at Boone Creek. If you have any questions for them they may be reached at 828-264-3689. For more information please visit their website at https://www.republicservices.com/locations/north-carolina/boone or stop by their customer service office at 300 Brook Hollow Road, Boone, NC 28607.
