TECH INFORMATION

TV / INTERNET AT UNIVERSITY OVERLOOK IS PROVIDED BY ATT.  IF YOU ARE HAVING ISSUES WITH YOUR SERVICE THEN PLEASE CONTACT ATT AT 1-800-331-0500 OR VISIT ATT.COM/ESUPPORT/. YOU MAY ALSO VISIT THE LOCAL BOONE ATT OFFICE AT 1126 BLOWING ROCK ROAD SUITE A, BOONE, NC 28607.

TV

The included TV package at University Overlook is AT&T U-verse TV Basic. There is one cable box in the living room space in each unit.

INTERNET

The included Internet package at University Overlook is AT&T Internet 25 Mbps. There is a dedicated modem supplied for each unit. The Internet package is not upgradeable and it is not possible to set up your own account through Charter.

WIFI

Wifi is included at University Overlook. Every unit has an AT&T supplied wifi router with separate wifi networks and log-ins.

TECH SUPPORT REQUEST

PROVIDED TECH

If you are having issues with any of the tech services or hardware provided by University Overlook then please contact AT&T for support. You may call them at 1-800-331-0500. Visit att.com/esupport/. Or visit the local Boone AT&T office at 1126 Blowing Rock Road Suite A, Boone, NC 28607.

If they are unable to assist you then you may submit a tech support request through your tenant portal. We will work with AT&T to help get your issue resolved. Please visit the Tenant Portal page to get started.

PERSONAL TECH

Boone High Country Rentals also offers tech support for tenant's personal tech items. If you are having issues with your personal computer, phone, tablet, wifi, printer, etc., then you may contact us through your tenant portal to request tech support.  There will be a fee for tech support related to personal items.
