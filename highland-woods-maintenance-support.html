MAINTENANCE INFORMATION

POWER

New River Light & Power provides the electric service to Highland Woods. If you have a power outage or issue please call them at 828-264-8621. For more information please visit their website at https://nrlp.appstate.edu/ or stop by their customer service office at 220 Winklers Creek Road, Boone, NC 28607.

WATER

Highland Woods is supplied by a private well for water.  If you have any water outages/issues then please call Boone High Country Rentals at 828-262-4646. Or you may come to our office at 895 Blowing Rock Road, Boone, NC 28607.

TRASH

Republic Services handles all trash/waste disposal at Highland Woods. If you have any questions for them they may be reached at 828-264-3689. For more information please visit their website at https://www.republicservices.com/locations/north-carolina/boone or stop by their customer service office at 300 Brook Hollow Road, Boone, NC 28607.
