TECH INFORMATION

TV / INTERNET AT FORREST EDGE IS PROVIDED BY CHARTER SPECTRUM.  IF YOU ARE HAVING ISSUES WITH YOUR SERVICE THEN PLEASE CONTACT CHARTER SUPPORT AT 833-267-6094 OR VISIT SPECTRUM.NET/SUPPORT. YOU MAY ALSO VISIT THE LOCAL BOONE CHARTER OFFICE AT 755 GEORGE WILSON ROAD, BOONE, NC 28607.

TV

The included TV package at Forrest Edge is Spectrum TV Select. There is one "community" cable box in the living room space in each unit. If you wish to have your own personal cable box in your room you will have to set up a personal account with Charter and pay for it yourself.

INTERNET

The included Internet package at Forrest Edge is Spectrum Internet Lite. There is a dedicated modem supplied for each unit. The Internet package is not upgradeable and it is not possible to set up your own account through Charter.

WIFI

Wifi is not included at Forrest Edge. Tenants must supply their own wifi router if they wish to have a wifi network in their unit.

TECH SUPPORT REQUEST

PROVIDED TECH

If you are having issues with any of the tech services or hardware provided by Forrest Edge then please contact Charter for support. You may call them at 833-267-6094. Visit spectrum.net/support. Or visit the local Boone Charter office at 755 George Wilson Road, Boone, NC 28607.

If they are unable to assist you then you may submit a tech support request through your tenant portal. We will work with Charter to help get your issue resolved. Please visit the Tenant Portal page to get started.

PERSONAL TECH

Boone High Country Rentals also offers tech support for tenant's personal tech items. If you are having issues with your personal computer, phone, tablet, wifi, printer, etc., then you may contact us through your tenant portal to request tech support.  There will be a fee for tech support related to personal items.
